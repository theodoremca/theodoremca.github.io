BucksTrybe

BucksTrybe - A Revolutionary Savings Platform

Embark on a savings journey like never before with BucksTrybe , a revolutionary platform developed with Flutter and Laravel. This cutting-edge app transforms communal saving by fostering transparency, accountability, and community building.

Key Features

  • Innovative Savings Group

    Download BucksTrybe from the AppStore or PlayStore for a transparent and accountable savings group experience.

  • User-Friendly Profile Setup

    Create your profile and complete your Know Your Customer (KYC) details in just two minutes.

  • Create or Join Savings Groups

    Initiate or join savings groups effortlessly, bringing you closer to a community-driven savings approach.

  • Mandate Structuring

    Set up your group's mandate, appointing initiators and approvers, and defining the criteria for the mandate to stand.

  • Flexible Contribution System

    Contribute money directly from the app or through a personalized group payment link, offering convenience and flexibility.

  • Real-Time Transaction Updates

    Receive email notifications for every transaction within your group, ensuring transparency and eliminating the risk of fraud.
