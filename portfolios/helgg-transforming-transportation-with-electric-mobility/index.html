Helgg - Transforming Transportation with Electric Mobility

Helgg,a micro-mobility company at the forefront of transforming the African transport industry. Helgg introduces an innovative shared network of electric vehicles, redefining how we experience and navigate our cities.

Key Features

  • Seamless Mobility Experience

    Download the Helgg App from AppStore or PlayStore for a seamless and eco-friendly transportation experience.

  • Micro-Mobility Redefined

    Creating a shared network of electric vehicles, Helgg introduces a new era in micro-mobility.

  • Zero Carbon Emissions

    Contribute to a sustainable future by choosing electric vehicles with zero carbon emissions.

  • Diverse Electric Fleet

    Explore a range of electric vehicles, including e-bicycles, e-scooters, and electric cars.

  • Helgg Mobile Wallet

    Access a network of e-bicycles, e-scooters, and e-cars with the Helgg Mobile Wallet for convenient payments.

  • Effortless Trip Management

    Scan a QR code, pay securely using the Helgg Mobile Wallet, and enjoy a safe and comfortable ride.
