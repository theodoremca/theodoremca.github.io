Calen360 - Sync calendars hassle-free

Calen360 allows you to sync your calendars with minimal effort, no more worrying about manually transferring your calendars!.

With just a few clicks, you can sync your work and personal calendars, and they'll keep in sync as your schedule changes. You are free to add as many personal and side gig calendars as you want.

You may tell Calen360 precisely how you want your blocked events to look to others, giving your coworkers as much or as little information as you like. You may also choose whether or not events outside of your working hours are prohibited. When personal events change, Calen360 automatically updates your work calendar. Calen360 also prevents your calendar from overflowing by designating your personal event blocks as busy only after you've accepted the invitation.
